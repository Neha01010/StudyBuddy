Quick Study Guide 🎓

🚀 How to start quickly:

  1. Type or paste your Google Gemini API Key into the header field and click Connect. The badge will turn green! 🟢
  2. Upload your PDFs inside the **Document Hub** in the left sidebar. Our local canvas renderer will render pages instantly! 📄
  3. Select whether you want to **Study Individually** (Single Doc) or **Study Together** (Combine All Docs) using the new selector switch! 📚
  4. Toggle tabs in the central panel to generate Custom Quizzes, Highly Detailed Mindmaps, or **PowerPoint Slidedecks**!
  5. Need direct answers or homework help? Chat with our AI tutor—it answers document-specific details as well as general knowledge queries! 📌
